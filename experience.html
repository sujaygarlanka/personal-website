Rewrite of networking layer for iOS SDK

  • After this rewrite, I add support for viewing the progress (#694) of and cancelling (#713) uploads and downloads

Audio and video viewing for the iOS preview SDK
  • Supports streaming instead of only downloading then viewing.

Support Creation and Downloading of Zip Files

  • Common use case is insurance companies who use Box to store their customer's claims, insurance info, etc.
